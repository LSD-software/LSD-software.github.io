INDEPENDENT GAME DEVELOPMENT MILAN

The Team & The Mission

LSD Software is an emerging indie game development collective founded by dedicated computer science students in Milan. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technical expertise and artistic storytelling, creating 100% free digital experiences for gamers worldwide.

Creative Trilogies & Lore

We specialise in crafting deep, narrative-driven experiences. Our portfolio includes indie titles and trilogies such as Arcana, The Machine, and In the Name of God. Every project features original dialogues, atmospheric low-poly graphics, and immersive soundtracks designed to push the boundaries of the indie genre.

Our Philosophy: Art for Art's Sake

In an industry driven by profit, LSD Software stands for free access to art. We believe games should be an accessible medium for everyone. That's why all our software, assets, and stories are available at no cost to our community — forever.

SUPPORT OUR VISION

Our work is fuelled by passion (and quite a bit of stress). While our games are free, we accept small donations to help keep the servers running and the coffee flowing.
