Who Are LSD?

Let's talk about LSD

LSD Team is a company made up of three young people studying computer science and looking to develop their skills. They do what they love: develop video games. They've created trilogies such as Arcana, The Machine, and In the Name of God. All stories, with dialogue, designed and created to immerse the player in this art form. We hope you enjoy playing our games, and it's all free! Just for you who appreciate our art... Thank you and have fun!
