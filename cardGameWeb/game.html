🃏 HOW TO PLAY

A card is shown on the left. Predict if the next card is HIGHER ▲ or LOWER ▼.

Set a BET before guessing. Win → coins ×2. Lose → lose your bet.

⚡ LSD Events appear randomly and twist the rules — tap the badge to re-read them.
