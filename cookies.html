Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Short version: We use only strictly necessary cookies/storage. No advertising cookies. No third-party tracking. You can clear all stored data via your browser settings at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. We also use localStorage — a browser storage mechanism similar to cookies but stored locally and never automatically sent to our servers.

2. What We Store and Why

Name / Key Type Category Purpose Expires
lsd_token localStorage Strictly Necessary Stores your JWT authentication token so you stay logged in between sessions. 7 days (JWT expiry)
lsd_user localStorage Strictly Necessary Caches basic user info (username, isGuest flag) to avoid repeated API calls. Same as token
lsd_trophies localStorage Functional Stores LSD Console trophy unlock state locally for offline play. Until browser data cleared
lsd_trophy_version localStorage Functional Version flag to reset trophy data if game updates change the schema. Persistent
lsd_nation_save localStorage Functional Saves your LSD Nation Builder game progress so you can continue later. Until browser data cleared
cookie_consent localStorage Strictly Necessary Records that you have seen and acknowledged this cookie notice. 1 year

3. Third-Party Storage

Google Fonts is loaded from Google's CDN. Google may set cookies related to font loading; these are outside our control. You can prevent Google Fonts loading by blocking fonts.googleapis.com in your browser or using a content blocker.

No other third-party cookies, analytics scripts (e.g. Google Analytics), or advertising networks are used on this site.

4. Your Choices

  • Clear localStorage: In your browser, go to DevTools → Application → Local Storage → select our domain → delete all keys.
  • Block cookies: Most browsers allow you to block cookies; note this may prevent login from working.
  • Opt out of Google Fonts: Use a browser extension that blocks Google CDN requests.

Since we only use strictly necessary and functional storage (no analytics or advertising cookies), a consent banner is not legally required under ePrivacy Directive Article 5(3) for these categories. However, we show a notice for transparency.

5. Cookie Banner

On your first visit you will see a brief notice at the bottom of the page. Clicking "Accept" or continuing to browse records your acknowledgement in localStorage under the key cookie_consent. No consent is required for strictly necessary storage.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

Questions about cookies: contact us.
