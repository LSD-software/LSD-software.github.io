🎮 HOW TO PLAY

Welcome, warrior. Two fighters enter — only one leaves standing.
Master your moves and crush your opponent!

🕹️ GAME MODES

VS CPU — Battle an AI. Choose Easy, Medium or Hard.
LOCAL 1v1 — Two players, one keyboard. Face your rival side by side.
ONLINE — Challenge a friend worldwide. Share a room code and fight!

🧙 CHOOSE YOUR FIGHTER

GRAX — Brutal warrior with bone-crushing power and regeneration.
YUKE — Swift mystic channeling cosmic energy. Fast and unpredictable.

⚔️ COMBAT BASICS

Punch — Fast, low damage. Quick to land.
Kick — Slower but heavy. Knocks opponents back far.
Block — Hold to reduce damage. Timing is everything.
Special Q — Your fighter's signature ranged attack.
Special E — A powerful ability unique to each fighter.

💡 TIP: Never stand still! Mix attacks to keep your opponent off balance.
🏆 GOAL: Drain your opponent's HP to zero. Last fighter standing wins!
