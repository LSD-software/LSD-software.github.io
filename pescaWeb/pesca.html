P-E-S-C-A

LSD SOFTWARE FISHING GAME

Welcome to LSD P-E-S-C-A — the retro fishing simulator by LSD Software. Cast your line, catch fish across 4 biomes, and climb the global leaderboard.

  • ◈ Dobloni — in-game currency, earned by selling fish
  • 4 Biomes — River (free), Sea (10◈), Ocean (20◈), Arctic (50◈)
  • Leaderboard — global ranking by total fish caught

Playing as guest? Progress saves in your browser. Log in from Settings to sync permanently to your account.

HOW TO FISH

FISHING MECHANICS

A vertical bar appears on the right side of the screen. A fish icon moves up and down — keep your red slider overlapping it for 3 seconds to catch it.

  • ↑ / ↓ or W / S — move slider (keyboard)
  • Mouse / Touch drag — drag slider on the bar
  • ▲ ▼ buttons — on-screen mobile controls

Rarer fish move faster and unpredictably — common fish bounce simply, rare fish burst sideways, mythic fish use erratic patterns.

RARITIES & RATES

FISH SPAWN SYSTEM
  • ● Common — fish 1–6 · 60% chance · sell for 1◈ each
  • ◆ Rare — fish 7–9 · 28% chance · sell for 5◈ each
  • ★ Mythic — fish 10 · 8% chance · sell for 10◈ each
  • 📼 VHS Tape — mysterious · 4% chance · one per biome

Every species you catch is recorded in your Field Log, accessible from the Shop.

SHOP & MAP

PROGRESSION SYSTEM
  • SHOP — sell fish for Dobloni, view your Field Log
  • MAP — spend Dobloni to unlock new biomes
  • Auto-save — every 30 seconds and on every catch
  • Settings ⚙ — audio, account login, reset progress
