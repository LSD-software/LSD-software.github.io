Welcome to LSD P-E-S-C-A — the retro fishing simulator. Cast your line, catch fish across 4 biomes, and climb the global leaderboard.

  • ◈ Dobloni — currency earned by selling fish
  • 4 Biomes — River (free) · Sea (10◈) · Ocean (20◈) · Arctic (50◈)
  • Leaderboard — ranked by total fish caught

Guest? Progress saves in your browser. Log in from Settings to sync to your account.

HOW TO FISH

MECHANICS

A vertical bar appears on the right. A fish moves up and down — keep your red slider overlapping it for 3 seconds to catch it.

  • ↑ / ↓ or W / S — keyboard
  • Mouse / Touch drag — drag on the bar
  • ▲ ▼ buttons — mobile controls
  • Pinch — zoom in/out
