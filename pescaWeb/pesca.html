LSD SOFTWARE — FISHING GAME

Welcome to LSD P-E-S-C-A — a retro fishing simulator. Cast your line, catch fish, collect rare species, and unlock mysterious biomes.

Your progress is saved automatically to your LSD account.

  • ◈ Dobloni — your in-game currency
  • C / R / M — Common, Rare, Mythic fish in your bag
  • 4 Biomes — River, Sea, Ocean, Arctic (unlock via Shop)

HOW TO FISH

A vertical fishing bar appears on the right. A gold zone (the fish) moves up and down. Your job is to keep your red slider overlapping the gold zone for 3 seconds to catch the fish.

FISH RARITIES

  • ● Common — sell for 1 Doblone. Fish 1–6.
  • ◆ Rare — sell for 5 Dobloni. Fish 7–9.
  • ★ Mythic — sell for 10 Dobloni. Fish 10. Counts for leaderboard!
  • 📼 VHS — mysterious object. One per biome.

Every fish you catch is recorded in your FIELD LOG.

SHOP & BIOMES

  • Sell fish from the SHOP to earn Dobloni.
  • Unlock new biomes in the MAP:
  • 🌊 Sea — 10 Dobloni
  • 🌀 Ocean — 25 Dobloni
  • ❄️ Arctic — 50 Dobloni

Progress is auto-saved every 30 seconds and on each catch.
