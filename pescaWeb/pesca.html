P-E-S-C-A

LSD Software Fishing Game

Welcome to LSD P-E-S-C-A, the retro fishing simulator by LSD Software. Cast your line, catch fish across 4 biomes, and climb the global leaderboard.

  • ◈ Dobloni — in-game currency earned by selling fish
  • 4 Biomes — River (free), Sea (10◈), Ocean (20◈), Arctic (50◈)
  • Leaderboard — ranked by total fish caught

Playing as guest? Progress saves in your browser. Log in from Settings to sync to your account permanently.

HOW TO FISH

A vertical fishing bar appears on the right. A fish moves up and down — keep your red slider overlapping it for 3 seconds to catch it.

  • ↑ / ↓ or W / S — move slider (keyboard)
  • Mouse / Touch drag — drag slider to position
  • ▲ ▼ buttons — mobile on-screen controls

Rarer fish move faster and more unpredictably.

FISH RARITIES & SPAWN RATES

  • ● Common — fish 1–6 · 60% chance · sell for 1◈
  • ◆ Rare — fish 7–9 · 28% chance · sell for 5◈
  • ★ Mythic — fish 10 · 8% chance · sell for 10◈
  • 📼 VHS Tape — mysterious · 4% chance · one per biome

Every species you catch is logged in your Field Log.

SHOP, MAP & SAVING

  • Go to SHOP → sell your fish for Dobloni
  • Go to MAP → spend Dobloni to unlock new biomes
  • Progress is auto-saved every 30s and on every catch
  • Use Settings ⚙ to manage audio, account or reset
