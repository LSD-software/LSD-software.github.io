Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Applies to: lsd-software.github.io and all sub-pages

Summary: We collect only what is strictly necessary to run the games and leaderboards. We do not sell your data. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

1. Who We Are

LSD Software is an independent game development project based in Milan, Italy. You can contact us at contact page or via the email listed there.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account data (optional): if you register, we store your chosen username, email address (hashed for login), and a bcrypt-hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Game scores: when you play logged-in, we store your best scores and match results (wins/losses) per game to display on the leaderboard.
  • Technical data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, request timestamp) stored temporarily for security and abuse prevention. These are not linked to your account.
  • Local storage: game progress, trophy data and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you are logged in.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and display your username on the leaderboard.
  • To show your rank and scores compared to other players.
  • To prevent abuse (rate limiting, ban of cheating accounts).
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling or sale to third parties.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your data based on:

  • Contract performance — providing the game account and leaderboard service you requested.
  • Legitimate interest — security logs and abuse prevention.
  • Consent — for optional cookies beyond strictly necessary ones (see Cookie Policy).

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you request deletion.
  • Game scores: retained as long as your account exists.
  • Server logs: automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Guest sessions: tokens expire after 24 hours and no personal data is retained.

6. Third-Party Services

  • MongoDB Atlas (database hosting, EU region) — MongoDB Privacy Policy
  • Render.com (server hosting, US) — Render Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages (static hosting) — GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Fonts (typography) — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN; see Google Privacy Policy.

No other third-party scripts, analytics platforms, or advertising networks are used.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR (if applicable to you) you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — delete your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time for consent-based processing.

To exercise any right, contact us via the contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds). API access requires JWT authentication. We apply rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks.

9. Children

Our games are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us for immediate deletion.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related requests: contact.html
